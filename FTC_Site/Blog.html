End of Season Wrap-Up

March 3rd, 2025

Because of the new website we didn't see reason to add to our new blog, but we thought we would at least add a recap of our first season.


For outreach we went door to door and asked companies individually if they would like to sponsor us. This helped with some more expensive parts we didn't have from previous teams such as a new control system and many, many, many, wires.

After debating using a claw or intake we decided on printing an intake as it would save us time. We also printed some smaller things such as spacers, mounts, and a battery mount.

We had many coding problems during competition which slowed us down. The encoders were not working properly which caused the entire robot to stop moving and basically functioning at all. We eventually made it manual.

The robot itself mainly consists of parts from previous teams which helped with most of our building, we did build it all ourselves. Our main advantage for our robot is using its arm to go to the high basket.
