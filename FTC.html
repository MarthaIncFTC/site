About FTC (FIRST)

The company that operates these robotics competitions is called FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) and they focus on youth STEM. They actually have 3 competitive robotics leagues: FLL (FIRST Lego League), FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge), and FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition).

The FLL competition is for grades 4-8; they build and program Lego robots to do missions.

The FTC competition is for high school students and mature middle school students; They build and program medium (microwave size) robots to do missions.

The FRC competition is for high school students only; They build and program big (refrigerator size) robots to do missions.
