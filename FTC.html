About FTC (FIRST)

The company that operates these robotics competitions is called FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) and they focus on youth STEM. They actually have 3 competitive robotics leagues:

FLL (FIRST Lego League)

For grades 4-8; they build and program Lego robots to do missions.

FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge)

For high school students and mature middle school students; they build and program medium (microwave size) robots to do missions.

FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition)

For high school students only; they build and program big (refrigerator size) robots to do missions.

Our team participates in FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge) - building and programming medium-sized robots to complete various missions and compete against other teams!
